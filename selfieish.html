IS 30 Self Portrait

emily brown

Self-portraits are the staple project of any art class. As soon as we can hold a pencil, we begin to draw ourselves. For this project, I was unclear as how to best depict myself. I've drawn myself many times throughout my life, enough times that I have a set method for how to formulate faces. Time and again I've used my method and become settled into it. This project forced me to reinvent that process as I translated myself not onto pen and paper, but into code.
